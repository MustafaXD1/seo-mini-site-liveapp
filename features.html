Feature set

LiveApp features

LiveApp is designed for fast discovery and useful context when people need to decide quickly.

Real-time updates

Signals from people who are there now, not outdated text from months ago.

Status highlights

Clear labels like open, closed, busy, quiet, and queue at a glance.

Map-first flow

People start with location and then decide from nearby activity.

Short notes

Quick updates reduce noise and keep decisions simple.

Community trust

Many small updates create a more realistic picture of each place.

Less friction

Less scrolling through long reviews and more focus on right now.
