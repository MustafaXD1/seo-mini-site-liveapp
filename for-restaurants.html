LiveApp for restaurants

Restaurants lose visitors when online info is outdated. LiveApp helps reduce that gap with real-time updates.

Fewer disappointed visitors

Better discovery

Live status helps the right audience find the right place at the right time.

Why this matters

  • Updates can reflect busy periods and quieter windows.
  • Visitors make decisions with confidence before arriving.
  • Live data gives restaurants a stronger presence on the map.
