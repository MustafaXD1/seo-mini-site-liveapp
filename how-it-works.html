How LiveApp works

LiveApp is built around short signals that appear directly on a map. The flow is simple and fast.

  1. Open the map near your location Start with nearby options and filter by what matters now.
  2. Read fresh status updates Signals like busy, quiet, open, closed, and queue help set expectations.
  3. Choose with less risk Decisions are based on live context instead of stale information.
